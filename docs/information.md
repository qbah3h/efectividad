Reward effort or outcomes: How should we measure a team?

Many of us say that we don't care how many hours someone works, just that the work gets done

But often the work can be stalled outside of someone's control

-New high priority projects for your team
-End user responsiveness
-Interdependent teams - other teams either unable to prioritize the work, issues in building it, etc

Measurements are one of my 4 key pillars. So how do you measure a team?

Many of us resort to pseudo measurements 
- Hours logged - but does this mean value?
- Tickets completed / story points completed - define done , and still does this mean value?
- OKRs completed - but what about the outside factors?


How do you measure your teams for success?
